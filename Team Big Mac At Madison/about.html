About Our Team

The team that never stops achieving.

Our team is in Division III of the United States Academic Decathlon. This means we participate as a small school with "465" students. We are representatives of our school, our city and our state. We are national contenders each year who always have our eyes on the prize. Our team has continuously dominated the state competition, thanks to stellar coaching, 14 times in a row and counting! We are comprised of "the best of the best" students in the state to be competitive. Our team is run by two sweethearted ladies, Paula Maxon being our head coach, and our assistant coach, Linda Lane.

Mrs. Paula Maxon She is the head coach of the Madison AD Team. She is assisting Will, a Honors decathlete, with a quiz.
Mrs. Linda Lane She is the assistant coach of the Madison AD Team.

Our Mission

Keep on Going

Our mission is to improve our performance as a team, and we will claim the trophy at nationals. We must increase our scores and skills each year to perform at the national level for consistent victories. Our team is about staying focused and determined, but also keeping a steady joy of learning. Our ultimate goal is to transform those who join the team into better students, before they move on.
