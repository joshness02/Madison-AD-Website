ABOUT THIS YEAR'S TOPIC

The topic for the United States Academic Decathlon 2016-2017 season is World War II. The topic will cover the seven subjects of Social Science, Science, Literature, Music, Art, Economic and Mathematics with a focus on the 1939-45 war. The United States Academic Decathlon was founded by Dr. Robert Peterson who was a prisoner of war by the Nazis in Germany when he came up with the idea of a decathlon. For more information, please check our History page.
